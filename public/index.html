      Shortcuts

      Press Modifier-Keys + Number (Ctrl + Shift + 1) on the keyboard to play sounds.

      Current possible numbers are 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 0.

      Modifier keys for shortcuts can be changed in the settings.
